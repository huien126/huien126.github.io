How to Protect us
from diseases

People become infected with many diseases throughout their lives and get better over and over again. It mostly makes us sick and infects others. Seriously, it even leads us to death.

This website provides basic information on how to effectively protect you and those around you from infectious diseases.

Social Distancing

For diseases that can be transmitted through person-to-person contact, maintaining physical distance from others is one of the most effective ways to prevent infection.

Get Vaccinated

Viruses are constantly changing, including the virus that causes COVID-19. These changes occur over time and can lead to the emergence of variants that may have new characteristics. Vaccines continue to reduce a person's risk of contracting the virus. Vaccines are highly effective against severe illness.

1 min

Wear A Mask

Wearing a mask is an excellent way to prevent infectious diseases that can be transmitted through the respiratory tract. In a pandemic situation caused by an infectious disease, it is desirable to wear a mask not only for myself but also for others.

Avoid crowds and poorly ventilated spaces

In an epidemic area, If you are in a crowded place, such as a restaurant, bar, fitness center, or movie theater, your risk of contracting an infectious disease increases exponentially. You should avoid these places as much as possible.

1 min

Wash Your Hands

One of the most important healthy habits to prevent the spread of germs is to clean your hands. Our hands can carry germs, so it is important to wash them often, even if they don’t look dirty.
