Social Distancing

Let's learn about social distancing, which is an effective way to prevent infection in areas where infectious diseases are endemic.

Stay 6 feet away from others

During an epidemic, physical distancing as a social commitment is a pretty effective way to stop the spread of the disease.

  • Inside your home :
    • Avoid close contact with people who are sick.
    • If possible, maintain 6 feet between the person who is sick and other household members.
  • Outside your home :
    • Remember that some people without symptoms may be able to spread virus.
    • Stay at least 6 feet (about 2 arm lengths) from other people, especially if you are at higher risk of getting very sick.
