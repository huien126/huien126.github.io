Avoid crowds and poorly ventilated spaces

Just avoiding places where the main source of infection is concentrated will definitely lower the chance of infection.

Avoid crowds and poorly ventilated spaces

  • Being in crowded places like restaurants, bars, fitness centers, or movie theaters puts you at higher risk for viruses.
  • Avoid indoor spaces that do not offer fresh air from the outdoors as much as possible.
  • If indoors, bring in fresh air by opening windows and doors, if possible.
